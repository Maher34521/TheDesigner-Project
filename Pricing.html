Pricing

The price of a clothing item is primarily determined by the clientâ€™s request, considering the following factors:


1. Number of Designs:

If the design is required on only one side (either front or back), the cost will be lower compared to designs that include both sides.


2. Design Complexity:

The price also depends on the complexity of the design. Simple designs that can be completed quickly will be more affordable, while intricate and time-consuming designs will cost more.


3. Type of Garment:

Pricing varies based on the type of clothing, such as short-sleeve shirts, long-sleeve shirts, or hoodies.


4. Delivery Cost:

Please note that delivery costs are covered by the client. However, since transportation costs are low, the overall price adjustment will be minimal.


Starting Price:

Prices start at 90,000 Rp as the base rate.
